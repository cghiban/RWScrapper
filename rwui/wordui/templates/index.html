A New Romanian Words Discovery System

The rapid evolution of World Wide Web has lead to major changes in the way we perceive and produce information. Moreover, the written content is now presented in a multitude of ways, many of them being subject to grammatical rule violations, ranging from spelling mistakes to style errors. Thus, Romanian language does not constitute an exception to this trend. Due to various factors, such as globalization and differences in social background of people who produce Web content, the Romanian language vocabulary has increased very rapidly. The rate and the model of this growth, even the approximate number of new lexems is unknown, all being subject to intensive research.
